[Rolex](https://www.rolex.com/en-us/watches/) is a luxury Swiss watch company. Rolex watches have been worn by heads of state (including Dwight Eisenhower), explorers, astronauts, athletes, and even James Bond.

Rolex has several notable models, including:
- Oyster Perpetual
- Day-Date
- Date Just
- Submariner
- Daytona
- Explorer
- Sea-Dweller
- Yacht Master
- GMT-Master
