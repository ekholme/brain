>Strategy is accepting that you are doing something better than the other and the other is doing something better than you. You have to pick your fight.

Axel Dumas, CEO of Hermes

## Opinion
I like this quote because it emphasizes the trade-off implicit in developing a strategy. Strategy is *not* finding a way to do everything; it's deciding where to prioritize and accepting that non-priorities are non-priorities.
