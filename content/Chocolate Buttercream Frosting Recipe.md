This works well with the [[Chocolate Cake Recipe]]

## Ingredients
- 3 sticks (339 g) unsalted butter
- 84g cocoa powder
- 568g (!) powdered sugar
- 1/2 cup milk
- 2 tsp vanilla extract

## Steps
- Cream butter & cocoa powder in a stand mixer
- Slowly add in powdered sugar and milk, continuing to mix
- Add vanilla extract
